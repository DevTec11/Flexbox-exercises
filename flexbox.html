Japan

Japan has plenty of attractions to visit, from the old capital of Kyoto to the new one that we all know, Tokyo. Across the entire country you can find different element of beauty that make the country such a staple for tourists. From the seal gazing in Okinawa to sight seeing the best anime locations, Japan is not fallling short in spectacles to behold.
